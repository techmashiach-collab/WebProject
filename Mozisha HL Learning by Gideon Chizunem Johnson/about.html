About SkillsForward Foundation

Transforming lives through FREE digital skills education since 2019

Our Mission

SkillsForward Foundation was founded in 2019 with a clear mission: to provide accessible, high-quality digital skills training to underserved youth communities across East Africa. We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to participate in the digital economy, regardless of their background or financial situation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every young person in East Africa has the digital skills needed to thrive in the 21st-century economy. Through our comprehensive curriculum and dedicated team of instructors, we strive to equip youth with practical skills that lead to meaningful employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.
